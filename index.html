About Me

Hello! I'm Ishani, and I'm passionate about sharing the transformative power of yoga. With over 5 years of dedicated practice and teaching experience, I've had the privilege of guiding countless individuals on their journey to holistic well-being.

My journey with yoga began during a challenging period in my life, seeking peace and strength. Since then, it has become an integral part of my life, helping me to connect deeply with my body, mind, and spirit. I believe that yoga is for everyone, regardless of age, flexibility, or experience level.

My teaching style focuses on creating a supportive and inclusive environment where students can explore their limits safely, cultivate mindfulness, and find joy in movement. I specialize in Vinyasa, Hatha, and Restorative Yoga, integrating breathwork and meditation into each session. Join me on the mat and discover the profound benefits that yoga can bring to your life!
